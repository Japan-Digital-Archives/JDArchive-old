Add social media counters to your blog so users can share your content and see how many others have shared.

== Description ==

Official plugin page with detailed instructions at [http://www.philhancox.co.uk/social-media-counters-wordpress-plugin](http://www.philhancox.co.uk/social-media-counters-wordpress-plugin)

This plugin adds social media counters to your blog posts from many services including Twitter, Facebook and the brand new Google +1. Full list of services so far:

* Twitter
* Facebook
* Google Buzz
* Google +1
* LinkedIn
* Digg
* StumbleUpon
* AddThis

*Flexibility*

* Configure buttons for top of posts, bottom of posts and excerpts.
* Select thin, compact buttons or vertical, tall counters.
* Enable on posts, pages and even custom post types if you have them.
* Add your Twitter username so shared Tweets have "via @yourname"

== Installation ==

1. Unzip and upload the ph-social-media-counters folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure settings through your WordPress admin at Settings > Social Media Counters
